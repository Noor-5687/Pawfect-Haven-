Parrots

Life Cycle

Parrots have an interesting life cycle that starts with eggs laid in tree cavities. The incubation period lasts around 18-30 days depending on the species. Chicks are born altricial, meaning they are blind and featherless, relying heavily on their parents. They fledge at around 8-12 weeks and continue to mature, with some species living up to 50 years in captivity.

Food Details

Parrots have a diverse diet consisting of seeds, nuts, fruits, berries, and vegetables. In captivity, a balanced diet includes high-quality parrot pellets, fresh produce, and occasional treats like nuts. It's important to avoid avocado, chocolate, caffeine, and alcohol, as these are toxic to parrots. Fresh water should always be available.
