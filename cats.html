Cats

Life Cycle

Cats go through several stages: "kitten, junior, prime, mature, senior, and geriatric". Kittens are playful and require socialization. As juniors, they develop their personalities. "Prime cats" are in their healthiest stage, while "mature and senior cats" need more health monitoring. Geriatric cats require special care and attention.

Food Details

Cats are "obligate carnivores", meaning they require a diet high in "animal protein". Quality "commercial cat food" provides balanced nutrition, but fresh meat, fish, and occasional treats like "cooked eggs" can be included. Ensure "fresh water" is always available and avoid toxic foods like "chocolate, onions, and grapes".
