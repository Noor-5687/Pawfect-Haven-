Chicks

Life Cycle

Chicks start as eggs, which hatch after about "21 days" of incubation. Hatchlings are covered with "soft down" and require warmth and care. As they grow, they enter the "juvenile stage", developing feathers and starting to explore. By "6-8 weeks", they become young adults, fully feathered and more independent.

Food Details

Chicks need a "high-protein starter feed" to support rapid growth. As they mature, their diet transitions to "grower feed", rich in grains and essential nutrients. Fresh greens, small grains, and occasional treats like "mealworms" can be added. Always provide "clean, fresh water" to support their health and development.
